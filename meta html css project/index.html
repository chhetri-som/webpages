About Us

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm offering a full suite of services in garder creation, landscaping and ongoing maintenance. Known for their creative vision and personalized service, Lucky Shrub also operates a boutique plant nursery featuring a diverse selection of indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of".

The company was founded by a husband and wife duo, Jason and Maria, who share a deep-rooted passion for plants and outdoor living. Jason, a skilled garden architect, leads all design products and manages the landscaping team. Maria oversees the company's marketing and day-to-day operations of the nursery, ensuring every client experiences a high level of service and plant quality

Services
